## 🎯 POSITIONING STATEMENT

**For songwriters who want to finish songs — not just start them.**

Lyriq is the first creative workspace that syncs your lyrics, voice memos, and beats in one flow. We're not a lyrics generator — we're a professional songwriting workspace. We help you finish *your* song, not write it for you.

### Strategic Positioning:
- **The "Burning Issue":** The copy attacks the specific pain point of fragmentation — songwriters toggling between Apple Notes for text and Voice Memos for audio (the "Two-App Toggle" friction)
- **The "Tesla Roadster" Approach:** Targeting "prosumers" (serious independent artists) who care more about workflow efficiency than polish
- **Differentiation:** LyricStudio = "Generator" (passive). Lyriq = "Workspace" (active/professional)

## 🔥 KEY SELLING POINTS — "The Solution"

### Feature 1: One Song, Two Lenses — Structure Your Chaos
Stop fighting your own brain. Start in **Flow Mode** to free-write without inhibition. When a structure emerges, toggle instantly to **Write Mode** to drag-and-drop your lines into Verses, Choruses, and Bridges. Your messy draft and your final structure live in perfect sync.

*Icon: Split Screen / Yin-Yang*

### Feature 2: Contextual Audio Capture — Write Inside the Music
Never toggle between Spotify and Notes again. Upload your beat directly to the project, set a **Custom Loop** for the chorus, and write while the groove plays. Record vocal ideas that attach directly to specific lines — so you never forget how the melody fits the words.

*Icon: Headphones + Waveform*

### Feature 3: The "Idea Bank" & AI Muse — A Co-Writer, Not a Ghostwriter
Stuck on a rhyme? Use our AI Muse to suggest rhymes that match your *context*, not just a dictionary definition. Save fleeting metaphors to your **Idea Banking Board** so they are ready when you need them. We help you finish *your* song, not write it for you.

*Icon: Lightbulb + Puzzle Piece*

### Feature 4: Your Rhyme Palette
Tap any word to see perfect rhymes, near rhymes, synonyms, and related concepts. Find the word that's on the tip of your tongue — in the language of your genre.

*Icon: Diamond / Layers*

### Feature 5: Export and Share
Copy lyrics, export as text or PDF. Take them to the studio, share with collaborators, or post them online.

*Icon: Download / Share*

## 🧪 THE SIGN-UP FLOW — "The Filter"
*Based on YC advice: Don't make sign-up too easy. Filter for high-intent users ("Ankits and Gustafs") who have a burning problem.*

### Step 1: The Hook
- **Input:** Email Address
- **CTA:** "Request Invite"

### Step 2: The "Burning Issue" Qualification
- **Question:** "How many unfinished song ideas are currently on your phone?"
  - [ ] 0–10
  - [ ] 10–50
  - [ ] "Too many to count / I'm overwhelmed" ← *Target User*

### Step 3: The "Payment" (Effort)
- **Prompt:** "We are building Lyriq to kill the 'Two-App Toggle.' In one sentence, what is the most annoying thing about your current songwriting process?"
- *(Why: This validates the problem AND provides qualitative data for your roadmap)*

### Step 4: The Commitment
- **Checkbox:** "I agree to use Lyriq to write at least one song draft this week and provide 15 minutes of feedback."
- *(Why: This ensures they are active users, not just lurkers)*

## 🎨 BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES

- **Tone**: Confident but not cocky. Like a talented friend who produces beats in their bedroom.
- **Language**: Conversational, direct. Avoid jargon. Never say "leverage" or "synergy."
- **Personality**: Creative, slightly irreverent, deeply respectful of the craft.
- **Core message**: "We help you finish *your* song, not write it for you."
- **Taboo words**: "Revolutionary," "game-changing," "disrupt," "10x"
- **Power words**: "Finish," "craft," "vibe," "capture," "flow," "voice," "sync"
- **Inclusive**: Gender-neutral. All genres. All experience levels. Hobbyists welcome.
- **Anti-positioning**: We are NOT a ghostwriter. We are NOT a lyrics generator. We are a professional workspace.
